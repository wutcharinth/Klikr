LANDING & SIGN-IN

A door so simple, anyone walks in.

One field for guests. One button for hosts. No signup screens, no friction.

Anyone joins with a code.

Audience scans the room QR or types six characters. No account creation, no email verification, no friction.

Hosts sign in with Google.

Single tap, single source of truth. Klikr uses Supabase Auth with Google as the only provider — no passwords to manage.

PRESENTER VIEW

Cinematic results, in real time.

Black canvas. Quiet typography. The numbers do the talking.

Lobby with QR.

Big code, big QR. Anyone scans, types a name, and they're in. No app downloads.

Live multiple choice.

Audience picks; the bar chart animates with each new vote. Aggregation happens client-side from a Supabase Realtime subscription.

Word cloud.

Words sized by frequency. Phantom audience members in the demo show what scale feels like.

Open-ended responses.

Stream of replies, newest first. Two-column layout keeps long responses readable.

Quiz with leaderboard.

Countdown ring, instant correct/incorrect feedback, leaderboard sorted by score formula round(1000 × (1 − response_time / time_limit)).
